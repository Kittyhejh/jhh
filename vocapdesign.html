VOCAP IOS APP DESIGN

You handy English vocabulary learning tool

INDEPENDENT PROJECT

UC BERKELEY EXTENSION // FALL 2017

WHAT IS VOCAP ?

Vocap is a mobile application that aims to help newcomers and ESL learners to learn English vocabulary in an innovative and efficient way.

GOALS 

Help ESL learners to study unfamiliar and new English words from real life content. Users can not only study the words and exam themselves from fresh and local content, but can also assimilate into US life better and faster by absorbing things that are happening around North America.

ROLES 

UX / UI Designer

KEY SKILLS 

User Research: Survey // Interviews // User testings
Design Research: Competitive analysis
Design strategies & Tools: Storyboard // Ad - hoc personas // Wireframing with Sketch // Prototyping with Adobe Illustrator & Sketch & Invision

Primary User Group

I conducted behavioral surveys and interviews with some of my ESL friends. The goals are to find out various English Vocabulary learning behaviors between different age group and status of ESL learners while they are browsing online English materials on the phone.

TARGET USERS

Newcomers, ESL learner who just start a life in the US and want to assimilate into US life faster. ESL beginners ( Study English less than 5 years, and seldom been to a English Speaking countries before)

Pain points:
1. They usually encounter a lot of new or local words when they just come to the US. They need to frequently look up words and find a good way to collect words they learn.
2. They learnt a lot of words from life in the US, but seldom get a chance to carefully study the words.
3. They want to assimilate into the US life faster.
