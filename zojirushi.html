UNDERSTAND WEB CONTENT 

 

Content strategy 

After understanding Zojirushi America's business goals, I conducted a qualitative audit, label audit and competitor site audit to analyze quality and effectiveness of Zojirushi’s web content.
The ultimate goal is to see if Zojirushi’s web content measure up to their current strategic goals.

 
Content Audit

I listed and record my observation of primary to tertiary web pages on a spreadsheet based on 12 audit terms.

 
lABEL AUDIT 

Besides reviewing content on Zojirushi America's website, I went further and deeper to audit labels on the website. The goal is to analyses quality and effectiveness of Zojirushi website’s labels.  

 
COMPETITOR WEBSITES AUDIT 

I also compared Zojirushi’s web content audit result with one of the competitor's website. It helps to study how competitor’s website content present differently to address the needs of target users. During the audit process, my instructor discuss content strategy with me and provide feedback to my audit observations

To study who Zojirushi website’s target users are, I conducted a demographic and behavioral survey with 10 participants.My goals are to identify target users’ demographic characteristics, discover their attitude and behaviors toward purchasing and using cooking appliances in life, and tasks they usually accomplish on a cooking appliances manufacturer website.  

 

PRIMARY PERSONA

UNDERSTAND THE WEB INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE 

 

Tree test & card sort & site map 

A cohesive user experience deos not live without a good navigation design. In this phase, I conducted an initial tree testing to evaluate discoverability and findability of current Zojirushi website’s IA. Then, based on the results, I did a card sorting with three target users to find patterns in categories during the activity. The goals of this phase are to optimize current web information architecture and the navigation system. 
 

 

DESIGN INSIGHTS

 

Facilitated access

Due to Inconsistent link treatments and dense wall of text,  users are difficult to scan for information of interest and neglect key information.To improve scannability and usability of the website, using consistent visual elements to help users look for services and help and obtain product information intuitively. 

 

find Information & help

 

Current Zojirushi America's web navigation system is not very efficient. Poor grouping and labeling of categories cause users can't find what they need. To solve this problem, create and apply useful navigation elements on the website based on the updated sitemap will help improve information's discoverability and findability.

 

show value of the brand  

 

The uniqueness of Zojirushi's website is offering the latest and valuable culinary and product information for customers every month. However, these types of information are poorly displayed on the site. Users do not find attractive and compelling to read and drop off immediately once they see the layout of the content display. Make a fun experience for users to scan through blogs and recipes may win back users' interest.
