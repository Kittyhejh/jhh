ZOJIRUSHI WEB REDESIGN

 

independent project 

uc berkeley extension // fall 2017

 CHALLENGE

As a traditional premium cooking appliances manufacture in the world, Zojirushi is losing their competitive power due to the growth of eCommerce.

GOALS

Redesign Zojirushi America website for meeting online customers' shopping and customer services needs and expectations. 

ROLES 

- Content Strategiest
- Information Architecture
- UX Designer 
- Individual Project // UC BerkeleyEx Fall 2017

KEY SKILLS 

Qualitative & Quality Content Audit
Card Sort
Tree Test
Site Map 
Wireframing with Balsamiq
Prototyping with Sketch & Invision 

DESIGN SPRINTS

  • UNDERSTAND THE BUSINESS

  • UNDERSTAND
    THE WEB CONTENT

  • Understand the users

  • understand the web ia & navigation

I conducted company Research, competitive analysis to  see how Zojirushi’s website compare with competitors when it comes to web design and to unveil the underlying intent of this project.
 

I did an qualitative content and label audit to analyses quality and effectiveness of Zojirushi’s website content.

To study who Zojirushi website’s target users are, I conducted a demographic and behavioral survey with 10 participants.Based on survey results, I created an ad-hoc persona to build empathy and specify a redesign vision for Zojirushi’s website.

A cohesive UX does not live without a good navigation design. I created tree testing and card sort
to evaluate discoverability and findability of current ZO website’s information architecture and find patterns in categories.

DESIGN INSIGHTS

 

Improve consistency

 Keep link treatments consistent and apparent on each page to help users scan for information of interest

 

apply intuitive navigation

 

Regroup categories and rename inaccurate labels to facilitate users access to the right information.

 

use simple layout  

 

 Engage users with culinary and product information by displaying the content on a simple and non-distrupted layout
